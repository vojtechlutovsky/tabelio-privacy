Privacy Policy for Tabelio

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Welcome to Tabelio. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the necessary data to provide you with a seamless experience across devices:

  • Account Information: Email address and basic profile info provided via Google or Apple Authentication.
  • User Content: Notes, sketches, images, and audio recordings that you create and save within the app.
  • Device Information: Basic technical data (OS version, device model) to help us troubleshoot bugs.

2. Data Storage and Third Parties

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage). We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data is used strictly for sync and backup purposes.

3. Data Deletion and Retention

We believe you should have full control over your data:

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete specific notes or your entire account directly within the App Settings.
  • Permanent Action: Deleting your account will immediately and permanently remove all your data from our servers.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Tabelio does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
